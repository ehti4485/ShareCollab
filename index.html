Features

on one network

No accounts, no uploads, no privacy risk. Just connect to the same Wi-Fi and get to work.

Auto Device Discovery

Finds all devices on your network automatically using mDNS. No IP addresses to type — just connect to the same Wi-Fi.

📝
Collaborative Editor

A rich-text editor where everyone types at the same time. Live cursors show who is editing and where — in real-time.

📁
Lightning File Sharing

Transfer files up to 500MB in seconds. Uses your full local network speed with no cloud bottleneck or upload limits.

📋
Clipboard Sync

Copy on your PC, paste on your phone. Clipboard content syncs instantly across all connected devices — no extra app needed.

💬
Group Chat

Discuss your work without leaving the app. A built-in group chat keeps all your team communication in one place.

🔒
PIN Protected Rooms

Lock your session with a 4-digit PIN. Only invited people can join — you have full control over who has access.

QR Code Join

Scan the QR code from any phone to join the session instantly. No typing, no IP addresses, zero friction.

Files and shared data expire automatically after a set time. Keeps your session private and your storage clean.
